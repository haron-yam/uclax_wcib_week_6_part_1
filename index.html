Coffee shops

L.A. is a city constantly on the move, even if we’re only moving at a glacial pace on the freeway. Our fuel to get through the day? Coffee, and lots of it. Whether it’s paired with a fantastic brunch or enjoyed solo, good java is as necessary to Angelenos as a solid taco stand. But where can you find a great cup? And what makes a good coffee shop? To help you find the perfect brew, we’ve sipped our way through the best spots in town.
